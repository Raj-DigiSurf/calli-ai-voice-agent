Welcome to Dalliance - An intimate Hair Studio & Urban Spa located in Oatley.

A $50 deposit is requested to secure your booking with us. This pre-payment will be put towards your service on the day of your appointment.

We kindly request a minimum of 24 hours notice to cancel all or part of your booking.

This $50 deposit is non-refundable only if you don't attend (no show) or give less than 24 hours required notice. We do understand that you may need to reschedule your appointment.

Please contact us to find a new suitable time and the $50 will then be carried forward to your new booking date.

As an Aveda Concept Salon, we only use Aveda Hair colour. Aveda is a naturally derived system which also has ammonia free options. Aveda colour leaves the hair feeling shiny, healthy & our salon has no nasty chemical processing aromas.

Our online booking times are a request only. A team member will be in contact to confirm & possibly slightly alter your booking time.
